Welcome, Backoffice!

As a Backoffice user, you can perform the following operations:

  • Change Role of a User: You can change a user's role from ENDUSER to PARTNER (or vice versa).
  • Change Account State: You can change the state of any user account (ACTIVE/INACTIVE).
  • Remove a User Account: You can remove any user account by their username or email, but only if the user has the role ENDUSER or PARTNER.
  • List Users: You can list all ENDUSER accounts and their details.
  • Change Account Attributes: You can modify, if your account status is ATIVADA, attributes for ENDUSER or PARTNER accounts, except for Username and Email.
  • Change Password: You can change your own password.
  • Logout: You can log out of your current session.
  • Create: You can create or update details about a project.
