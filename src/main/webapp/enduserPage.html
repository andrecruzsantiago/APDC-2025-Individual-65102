Welcome, Enduser!

As an Enduser, you can perform the following operations:

  • List Users You can list some attributes of account with the role ENDUSER, having a public perfil and ATIVADO state.
  • Change Password: You can change your own password.
  • Change Account Attributes: You can modify your own account attributes, except for Username, Email, Role, and Status.
  • Logout: You can log out of your current session.
