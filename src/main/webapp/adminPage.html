Welcome, Admin!

As an Admin, you can perform the following operations:

  • Change Role of a User: You can change the role of any user to any other role.
  • Change Account State: You can change the state of any user account to any state.
  • Remove a User Account: You can remove any user account by their username or email.
  • List Users: You can list all users and see their full details.
  • Change Account Attributes: You can modify any account's attributes, including role and status.
  • Change Password: You can change your own password.
  • Logout: You can log out of your current session.
